# Dictionary

The dictionary is the **per-person recall** surface. It answers the question
*"What do I already know about this person?"* — the one you ask right before a
call or a follow-up.

## Purpose

- Browse and **search** everyone you have met.
- Open a person to see their **description**, **labels**, and a history of
  **events** — when you met, the context, who else was there, and what you
  discussed.

## Key elements

- A searchable grid of people (name, labels, event count).
- A person detail view: avatar, description, labels, and a list of events with
  date, context (e.g. the conference), and co-attendees.

## Notes

- All content here is produced by the Chat surface — there is no edit button.
- Clicking a person from the Timeline surface or Follow ups surface deep
  links into their detail view.

## Future idea

A **company / context map** — grouping people by organization or project — would
help managers who think "who do I know at Company X?" rather than person by
person. Noted as a candidate surface, not built yet.
