# Follow-ups

Follow-ups are the **commitment tracker**. For relationship-building, remembering
a small promise ("I'll send them the doc", "they said they'd review it") is often
higher value than remembering a name. The agent extracts these from braindumps
automatically.

## Purpose

- Surface open commitments — **what you owe** and **what others owe you** — with
  due dates where known.
- Keep relationships warm by making promises impossible to forget.

## Key elements

- A list of open commitments grouped before completed ones.
- Each item shows the commitment, the person (deep-linking to the
  Dictionary surface), who owns it, and an optional due date.

## Behavior

- Items are **created and completed via the Chat surface**, consistent with
  the chat-only editing model. The list itself is read-only — you tell the agent
  "done, sent Ada the doc" and it updates.

This surface came out of the product's rapport goal — see Intents.
